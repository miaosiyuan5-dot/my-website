The Making

Four Pillars of Zisha Craft

紫砂工艺四步骤

From raw mountain ore to a vessel that transforms every pour — a journey measured in days, guided by generations of knowledge.

01

Mining Clay

选矿取泥

Only ore from the Huanglongshan deposit in Yixing passes our strict selection — rich in iron, silica, and alumina for optimal porosity.

02

Hand Throwing

打泥片围身

The artisan beats the clay into even slabs by hand, then coaxes them into form without a wheel — an ancient technique unchanged for centuries.

03

Engraving

刻绘题字

While the clay is leather-hard, the artisan carves calligraphy or landscapes with bamboo tools — each stroke deliberate, each line irreversible.

04

Firing

龙窑烧制

Fired at 1180°C in a traditional dragon kiln, the clay vitrifies into the dense, slightly textured surface that gives Zisha its legendary tea-aging properties.

Our Story

A Living Tradition,
Brought to the World

百年传承 · 走向世界

Dingyuan was founded with one mission: to bring the world's finest Yixing Zisha teapots to tea lovers everywhere. We work directly with master artisans who have inherited their craft through family lineages stretching back multiple generations.

Every teapot we offer is certified authentic — made from natural, unblended Zisha ore, shaped entirely by hand, and engraved with original artwork. We accept no compromises, because neither does the clay.
